Welcome to the Dice Roll Game! This exciting game is a twist on the classic dice game, where your strategy and luck will determine your success. The goal is to be the first player to reach a total score of 100 points.

Gameplay Overview:

  1. The game is played between two players, Player 1 and Player 2.
  2. Player 1 starts the game. To begin their turn, they need to click on the "Roll Dice" button.
  3. When the "Roll Dice" button is clicked, a virtual dice will be rolled, and a number between 1 and 6 will be generated. This number represents the points earned during that roll.
  4. After each roll, the points earned are added to the player's current score. The current score starts at 0 at the beginning of the turn.
  5. Player 1 can choose to roll the dice multiple times during their turn by clicking the "Roll Dice" button. The points from each roll will be added to the current score.
  6. However, if a roll results in the number 1, the player's current score will be reset to 0, and it becomes Player 2's turn. So, be cautious and strategic with your rolls!
  7. To secure the points earned in their current turn, Player 1 can click the "Hold" button. The current score will be added to their total score, and it becomes Player 2's turn.
  8. Player 2 follows the same rules as Player 1, rolling the dice to accumulate points or choosing to hold to secure their points.
  9. The game continues to alternate between Player 1 and Player 2 until one of the players reaches a total score of 100 or more.

Winning the Game:

The player who first accumulates a total score of 100 points or more is the winner! So, use your dice-rolling skills and strategic decision-making to outplay your opponent and claim victory.
